## What Knowlery Is

Knowlery is an Obsidian plugin for people who want their notes to become more than a pile of markdown files. It creates a shared working layer between you, your vault, and AI coding agents.

Your free-form notes remain yours. Knowlery gives agents a structured place to work: `entities/`, `concepts/`, `comparisons/`, `queries/`, `KNOWLEDGE.md`, `SCHEMA.md`, `INDEX.base`, skills, rules, and platform configuration.
