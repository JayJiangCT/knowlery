One mechanism, two names in circulation ("review gates" internally,
"approval gates" in the blog draft) — recorded as aliases, deliberately one
page. Every item is explicitly approved or flagged; there is no approve-all;
gate state survives sessions. The [[Release Train]] consumes this gate.
