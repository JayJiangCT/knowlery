# Observability Starter Pack

## Concepts

* [Four Golden Signals](/concepts/four-golden-signals.md) - Latency, traffic, errors, and saturation — the four golden signals of observability from the Google SRE book.
* [OpenTelemetry Semantic Conventions](/concepts/opentelemetry-semantic-conventions.md) - Naming conventions for spans, attributes, and instrumentation so telemetry stays comparable across services.
* [RED Method](/concepts/red-method.md) - Rate, errors, duration — a request-oriented subset of the golden signals for service dashboards.
